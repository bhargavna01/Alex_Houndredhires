## Methodology for Selection

1. **Relevance Filter**: Must directly address AI-powered SEO content production
2. **Practitioner Test**: Must show evidence of building/running actual AI content systems
3. **Platform Diversity**: Mix of YouTube creators, LinkedIn thought leaders, podcast hosts
4. **Content Freshness**: Active in 2025–26 with recent content
5. **Unique Angle**: Each brings a distinct approach or perspective
6. **Actionability**: Content provides workflows, prompts, or systems that can be replicated
