## Content

Fable 5, the most capable model available, came back online July 1 and is included in Pro, Max, and Team plans only through July 7. After that, using it costs paid credits. That deadline surfaces the question most AI companies are avoiding: once your team runs on powerful, expensive, autonomous tools, who decides when to reach for them, who approves what, and how do you keep any of it accountable? Eric Siu breaks down the operating model behind Single Grain, the autonomy thresholds that let people act without a permission chain, how he keeps a company full of agents from going off the rails, and where a model like Fable 5 fits before the window closes. For founders and operators running a business on top of AI, not just building with it.
